Originally, our project idea was a shopping assistance application. The application would include AR tools to allow the user to view clothing and furniture with their phone or mobile device, as well as, text, image, and speech-to-text searching.

However, after meeting with the professor, we have narrowed our focus to attempt to create a clothing shopping AR app. This app will allow users to view clothing with their phone and see how it will look on them. The app will also attempt to make clothing and color suggestions.
